SP DGDD/2D Platformer Game

Lastest Update: 2018

Game Description

Play in the shoes of our young brave hero, Atlas, who seeks out on an epic quest to end Modred's conquest over Falguard and restore it back to its former glory. Wield the trusty hammer and use it to rebuild the hope once lost in the Kingdom of Falguard by bringing judgment to the different enemies that stand in your way.

Game Features

  • Challenging Gameplay without the ability to jump.
  • Beautiful pixel art and animation.
  • Features 2 Bosses with their own unique attack patterns.
  • Explore and find collectibles hidden that consist of lore regarding Falguard.
Design Document

Project Information

Restore of Falguard is a 2D Platformer assignment made as part of a studio project assignment. Developed in a team of 2 consisting of a programmer and an artist, this was the first game that I have ever made and it was the project that shifted my gears from wanting to be an artist to a programmer.

I worked as the game designer and programmer of this project despite having little to no knowledge in the field of programming. A lot of the fundamentals such as loops were not yet taught and as such, required me to do my own research and consultation to develop this game. Despite having little to no knowledge, I managed to develop simple enemy AIs, design boss fights, create a collectable system and even develop a simple combo attack system.
