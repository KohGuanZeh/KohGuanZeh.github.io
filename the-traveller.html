SP DGDD/3D Puzzle Game

Lastest Update: 2019

Game Description

Explore the Forgotten Ruins in the guise of our cute, cuboidal friend - The Traveller. With the ability to flip dimensions, solve the puzzles in each level to proceed further and who knows what secrets you can find!

Game Features

  • Simple Controls, Challenging Puzzles.
  • Solve puzzles quickly and attain a High Score.
  • Traverse between Realms to make your way to the end.

Project Information

The Traveller was the first 3D Game that I developed as part of a game programming module assignment. Although a base template and scripts provided for the movement of the cube, we still had to design and develop our own mechanics for this puzzle game. In addition, there were also minor bugs and issues with the scripts provided which required us to do our own bug fixing.

Some notable skills and knowledge I picked up in this project included the use of delegates and raycasting, which were core to developing fluid camera and player movement in the game.
