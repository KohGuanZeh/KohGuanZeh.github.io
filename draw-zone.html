Game Description

Get ready to be bamboozled as you draw out your solutions across 12 different puzzles Draw Zone has to offer. A 2D Puzzle Game with the added twist of different gravity zones and challenging level designs, this Game will definitely test your creativity and logical thinking in your pursuit of a perfect score across all levels.

Draw Zone (.APK)

Project Information

Draw Zone is a physics mobile game as part of an individual game programming assignment and was something I developed from the ground up, including the design and mechanics of the game. As this was one of the first few mobile games that I developed, I did learn a lot about mobile development in Unity, from touch input controls to exporting the game as an APK.

From the perspective of a designer of this game, I feel that it is one of my best packaged works, which although simple, managed to fit well with the theme, gameplay and aesthetics, inclusive of the music that I sourced online. Definitely one of my best packaged projects during my diploma studies.
