Lastest Update: 2020

Application Description

My Little Tokyo is an AR Application featuring a small town packed with a myriad of different interactions for users to discover. Enjoy the relaxing and iconic tunes of Animal Crossing while discovering the surprises that this town has in store. From random sound effects to videos playing on the flying television, this little town is sure to bring a great deal of adventure.

Application Features

  • Mobile AR Application.
  • Up to 21 Unique Interactions.
  • Interactions vary from playing Sound Effects, to Animations and Videos.
  • In-Built Video Player on a Flying Television.
  • Secret Interaction that transitions the Scene from Day to Night.
  • Secret Interactions to be found during the Night.

Project Information

My Little Tokyo is the first AR application that I have developed for my programming assignment with the use of Unity and Vuforia. I wanted this application to be a mini adventure for its users, to appreciate the intricacies and bring life to astounding 3D models. Apart from learning how to work with Vuforia,I have also learnt how to implement interfaces and the concept of polymorphism, which streamlined the flow of developing the different interactions packed in this mini adventure.
